About Lime3D

Lime3D is an open-source, standalone graphics library designed to provide GPU drivers and support for modern graphics APIs. Unlike other libraries, Lime3D is entirely original and does not rely on pre-existing frameworks.

Our goal with Lime3D is to offer developers a clean and powerful toolset for integrating GPU acceleration and creating complex graphical applications.

Featured APIs

At the moment, we are proud to showcase:

OpenKVDR

OpenKVDR is our proprietary **GPU renderer** included with Lime3D. It supports advanced techniques like ray tracing, 3D model rendering, and other complex graphical features.
